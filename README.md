## 🔥 What I've shipped

### 🧠 AquaCalm AI — Psychotherapist GPT
> *"I deal with anxiety myself, and mental health support in India is hard to access. So I built the support I needed."*

An AI-powered mental health companion with voice input/output — listens, responds, and speaks. Built & competed at **Cal Hacks 12.0**.

`Python` `React` `OpenAI API` `Google TTS/STT` `Google API`

### 🏢 BSR — Business Suite
A complete accounting & billing system. Day-to-day sales, monthly/yearly reports, invoicing, credit tracking — everything a business needs, in one place. Built to replace manual records for a real business.

`TypeScript` `MySQL` `Google Sheets API` `Google Cloud`

---

### 🛒 Family Business E-Commerce Platform
Full-stack e-commerce app with product listings, cart, checkout, and order management. Built because paying Shopify fees for a family business made zero sense.

`C#` `ASP.NET` `Flask`
